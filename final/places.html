Awesome places to ride at in Arizona!

Near Phoenix Arizona, there is many wonderful places to ride dirt bikes, and any offroad vehicle, without having to travel very far. You can spend a day or a whole weekend at any one of these amazing places.

Arizona Cycle Park With 4 tracks available for use at any time, they make a great stop if you are into hitting the track.
Ehrenberg Sandbowl The Ehrenberg Sandbowl offers 10 miles of trails to ride on so you could never get bored there. They also offer RV hook ups and camping, so you can enjoy extended stays to see all of the trails while you are there.
Agua Caliente Agua Caliente offers a more laid back experience. With their amazing scenery and 25 miles of trails, you can always expect to have a good time. This trail is also best explored in the winter and spring months as they do tend to get hotter climate than most areas.
Outlaw OHV Trail This 6.3 mile long trail is located closer to Sedona and offers a fun time for all skill levels. It also offers a chance to see some amazing wildlife.
