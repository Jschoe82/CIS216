Important Supplies for Riding Track and Trail.

Here I will cover some important riding gear to bring with on any trip to the track or trails of Arizona.

Helmet

Number one most important piece of gear is the helmet. Most places will require you to wear one any way so it is always good to have one with you.

Chest Protector

The chest protector is an optional piece but I highly recommend it. If you ever get behind someone else who is riding and their tire catches a rock, if can fly right at your chest and this piece of gear will save you from a lot of pain.

Boots

Dirt bike boots are special boots unlike normal work boots. They help keep your ankle from flexing too far an causing a sprain or even worse, a break. I highly recommend these when riding anywhere.

Water!

This is the most important item when riding anywhere in Arizona, water! Do not forget this when out and about in the desert because it does get very warm and hydration is very important!
